Analytical Ability – Part 6: Data Interpretation

Created by: CSE Learning Essentials

Passing Score: 8

Directions: Study the given pie graph carefully and answer the questions below:

1. The largest component of the school’s budget is:

  • A) Classroom Supplies
  • B) Technology and Equipment
  • C) Teacher Salaries
  • D) Student Programs and Activities
  • E) Administrative Costs

2. The smallest component of the school’s budget is:

  • A) Classroom Supplies
  • B) Technology and Equipment
  • C) Teacher Salaries
  • D) Student Programs and Activities
  • E) Administrative Costs

3. If the total school budget is ₱25,000,000, the total allocation for Technology and Equipment would amount to:

  • A) ₱2,500,000
  • B) ₱3,000,000
  • C) ₱5,000,000
  • D) ₱1,200,000
  • E) ₱200,000

4. If the total school budget is ₱25,000,000, what is the allocation for Student Programs and Activities?

  • A) ₱2,500,000
  • B) ₱500,000
  • C) ₱2,000,000
  • D) ₱3,000,000
  • E) ₱1,500,000

5. If the total school budget is ₱25,000,000, the combined amount of "Teacher Salaries" and "Building Maintenance" expenses would amount to:

  • A) ₱14,500,000
  • B) ₱17,000,000
  • C) ₱15,000,000
  • D) ₱16,000,000
  • E) ₱18,000,000

6. If the budget for Teacher Salaries was to be cut by one-third, how much will be left? (Total budget: ₱30,000,000)

  • A) ₱7,000,000
  • B) ₱8,000,000
  • C) ₱9,000,000
  • D) ₱10,000,000
  • E) ₱12,000,000

7. If the budget for Teacher Salaries was to be increased by 25% of its current total, how much of the budget would be allocated for Teacher Salaries? (Total budget: ₱40,000,000)

  • A) ₱22,500,000
  • B) ₱28,000,000
  • C) ₱10,000,000
  • D) ₱15,000,000
  • E) ₱18,000,000

8. If the allocation for "Student Programs and Activities" was decreased by 15%, what would be the new amount? (Total budget: ₱40,000,000)

  • A) ₱2,800,000
  • B) ₱3,200,000
  • C) ₱6,000,000
  • D) ₱3,500,000
  • E) ₱2,720,000

9. If the total school budget is ₱25,000,000, the combined allocation for "Classroom Supplies" and "Technology and Equipment" would amount to:

  • A) ₱4,000,000
  • B) ₱3,500,000
  • C) ₱3,000,000
  • D) ₱5,000,000
  • E) ₱5,500,000

10. If the budget for "Classroom Supplies" is increased by 20%, what would be the new allocation? (Total budget: ₱35,000,000)

  • A) ₱1,200,000
  • B) ₱1,500,000
  • C) ₱4,200,000
  • D) ₱7,000,000
  • E) ₱10,500,000
